Harry Potter

The Harry Potter Books is a series of seven Fantasy Books containing over 700 Characters. The main Characters are Harry Potter, Hermione Granger, Ronald Weasley and Voldemort. Harry, Hermione and Ron all attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. There are 11 different Wizardry School around the world but can only name 8. Ilvermorny, Uagadou, Castelobruxo, Koldovstoretz, Durmstrang, Mahoutokoro, Beauxbatons and Hogwarts. Harry Potter parents, Lily and James Potter, Met at Hogwarts and soon after they graduated, they got killed by Voldemort. Voldemort tried to kill Harry, who was a baby at the time, but got away with a scar above his right eyebrow shaped as a lightning bolt. Voldemort died that night and since then everybody knew Harry as the Boy who Lived. Harry was raise by his Aunt Petunia, her partner, Uncle Vernon, and their son, Dudley. Growing up Harry didn't know he was a wizard until he turned 11 and Hagrid, Gamekeeper at Hogwarts, turned up and took him to buy his things he need for school. Hermione Granger comes from a Muggle(non-wizard folk) and Ronald Weasley is a Pureblood (Both Parents and wizard folk). Hermione Granger is Smart, gets straight A's and has no other siblings. Ronald Weasley has 6 siblings and Ron can be described as Loyal and Clumsy.
